Giovanni Bellini,1510
Christ Carrying the Cross
Who painted this picture? When the Museum purchased this painting in 1940, it was considered an original work by the Venetian master Giovanni Bellini. But as time passed and after a cleaning in 1986, several art historians questioned this attribution. After recent research, we now feel more confident that Bellini indeed worked on the painting. Famous and successful, Bellini had a very large workshop, which was typical in Venice during the Renaissance.
